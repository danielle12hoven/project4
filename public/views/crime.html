FAQ About New York


  • Do I need a passport to travel to the United States?

    Yes. You need to be in posession of a legal/valid passport (expiration date that remains valid for your entire stay in the US).


  • Do I need a VISA to move to the United States?

    Stays < 90 days

    Yes, for any stay in the US that is less than 90 days you will need to acquire an ESTA travel pass (this is the travel VISA).

    You can find more information and register yourself here via ESTA: http://www.allesamerika.com/esta-amerika.html

    Stays > 90 days

    For stays longer than 90 days (internships, study abroad, and full-time jobs) you will need to acquire a VISA.
